Becek Rice

From the unique name, many people are couriuus to tasted a Becek rice. Nasi Becek is a mixture of curry and goat satay that is poured over a bowl of white rice.

Kenul Satay

Kenul satay is goat satay seasoned with grated young coconut, chili and sugar which is fried over medium heat. Kenul satay can only be found in Lumpang Kuwik village, JatiKalen sub-district has a sweet spicy taste

Asem Asem Kambing

This Nganjuk specialty food made from mutton uses Kedondong leaves. These tamarind leaves of kedondong goats are often found in the Ngluyu area, close to the tourist attraction of Margo Tresno Cave in Ngluyu District, North Region of Nganjuk Regency.

Tumpang Rice

Tumpang rice is a culinary mainstay in East Java, especially Nganjuk, Kertosono, and Kediri. Tumpang rice is rice doused with overlapping chili sauce. Tumpang Rice made from tempe that has been fermented for two or three days.

Sedudo Corn Rice

Corn rice culinary has become an icon at the tourist location of Sedudo Waterfall. Apart from its distinctive taste, traditional food has indeed been sold for a long time. In fact, there are traders who last for decades.

Dumbleg

Dumbleg is a traditional food from Nganjuk. This sweet and sticky food is made from rice flour, palm sugar and coconut milk. This special food with a sticky taste is easily found in the Gondang area.
