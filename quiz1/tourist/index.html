Ngetos Temple

Some famous historical temples are scattered in Nganjuk Regency, which is suitable as a vacation destination. One of them is called Ngetos Temple! As the name suggests, it belongs to Ngetos Sub-District. The exact location is in Selopuro Village and it features easy accessibility. Due to the popularity and nice access, the number of visitors is high for sure. Not to mention it resides in the north slope of Wilis Mountain. That means visitors can enjoy sightseeing during the visit, as the panoramas are great up there. Plus, the temple holds an interesting history to learn.

Margo Tresno Cave

Natural attractions never disappoint tourists. It is because people can enjoy a peaceful nuance and beautiful panoramas during the visit. Another benefit is the adventure opportunity! In this case, Nganjuk Regency offers Margo Tresno Cave for nature lovers. It is located in Sugih Waras Village at Ngluyu Sub-District. This mystical cave offers more than beauty, in fact. It is because visitors can enjoy other things like caving, photography, and exploration. As long as everyone knows the route, they can safely explore the site! The atmosphere is merrier if they come with either friends or family members, though.

Anjuk Ladang Museum

Anjuk Ladang Museum Located in the city of Nganjuk, to the east of the Nganjuk City Bus Terminal, it contains objects and cultural heritages in the Hindu, Doho and Majapahit era in the Nganjuk Regency area. Besides that, the Anjuk Ladang Inscription was kept, which was the forerunner to the founding of Nganjuk Regency.
This museum is divided into several parts of the building. The front of the joglo is a place to store the Anjuk Ladang inscription, the main building as a storage area for jars, bowls, shadow puppets, bells, masks and so on.

Roro Kuning Water Fall

Bajulan Village in Nganjuk Regency is known for its waterfalls. No wonder tourists keep coming to that area, especially when exploring Loceret Sub-District. As for the reference, there is the famous Roro Kuning Waterfall. This stunning waterfall resides on Wilis Mountain and it is neighboring to other popular falls like Pacoban Lawe and Pacoban Ngunut. The panorama and nuance are superb, but tourists must spend some stamina and time in order to reach the location. Roro Kuning Waterfall is popular due to many reasons, actually. Still, two major allures are the beauty and historical value.

Sedudo Water Fall

Sedudo waterfall is situated in the Ngliman village, Sawahan district, within Nganjuk Regency, East Java, Indonesia. The waterfall is located some 30 km south of the district’s capital, Nganjuk, and lies at an altitude of 1,438 metres above sea level, with a drop of approximately 105 metres. Sedudo is close to the base of Mount Wilis, a volcanic massif with no recorded history of eruptions.

Kali Bening Reservoir

Bening Widas (Kali Bening) Reservoir, is a reservoir located in Saradan District, Madiun Regency, East Java or 40 km to the east of Madiun City. Bening Widas Reservoir was built by damming the flow of Kali Bening and its tributary, Kali Petung, so the name of this dam is called Bening Dam.
